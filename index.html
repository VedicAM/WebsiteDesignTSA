Mango Lassi is a rich and creamy yogurt-based drink blended with ripe mangoes, a touch of sugar, and fragrant cardamom, creating the perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess. Our Mango Lassi stands out as the best in the state, made with the freshest Alphonso mangoes and authentic, velvety yogurt for an irresistibly smooth texture. Each sip bursts with tropical flavor, making it a refreshing treat that keeps customers coming back for more. Whether paired with a spicy meal or enjoyed on its own, our Mango Lassi is an unbeatable delight!

Our Pan-Fried Paneer is a crispy, golden delight that melts in your mouth with every bite. Made from the finest, fresh paneer, it's lightly seasoned with aromatic spices and seared to perfection, creating a crispy exterior while keeping the inside irresistibly soft. Known as the best in the state, our Pan-Fried Paneer is a customer favorite, offering the perfect balance of rich, creamy texture and bold, savory flavors. Whether enjoyed as an appetizer or a side, this dish is a must-try for all paneer lovers!

Our Daal is a comforting, hearty dish made with the finest lentils slow-cooked to perfection with aromatic spices. Simmered with garlic, ginger, and a hint of ghee, it delivers a rich, creamy texture with deep, earthy flavors. Widely regarded as the best in the state, our Daal is a customer favorite, offering warmth and nourishment in every spoonful. Whether paired with rice or enjoyed on its own, this classic dish is a true taste of home-cooked goodness!
